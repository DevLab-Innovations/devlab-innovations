Summa Ledger

Built for the way you live and work

Every feature is designed for anyone who wants clarity, speed, and complete privacy over their finances — freelancers, side hustlers, and everyday budgeters alike.

Core

Expense Management

The foundation — fast, flexible, and fully offline.

📋

Expense Logging

Add expenses in seconds. Set the amount, category, date, and notes — then get back to work. Full search and filter across your entire history.

🏷

Custom Categories

Organize expenses using built-in Schedule C categories or create your own. Every category maps cleanly to tax deduction types.

📷

Receipt Capture & OCR

Photograph a receipt and the app reads the merchant, date, and amount for you. Attach photos directly to any expense for a complete audit trail.

🔍

Smart Search & Filters

Instantly find any expense by keyword, category, date range, or amount. Multi-select filters let you slice your data any way you need.

Mileage

Never miss a deduction

The IRS allows a standard mileage deduction for business travel. Summa Ledger makes it effortless to claim every mile.

🚗

Trip Logging

Log any trip with start/end locations and distance. Add a purpose and client — everything you need for a clean IRS-compliant mileage log.

📈

Automatic Deduction Calculation

Summa Ledger applies the current IRS standard mileage rate automatically. See your total deduction value update in real time as you log trips.

🖽

Mileage Reports

Export a complete mileage log in CSV or PDF — formatted exactly as tax preparers and accountants expect to receive it.

🎯

Purpose Tracking

Tag each trip as Business, Medical, Charity, or Personal. Different trip types use different IRS rates — Summa Ledger handles the math.

Reports & Sharing

Clarity at tax time and beyond

Powerful reports and seamless accountant sharing make year-end easy.

📊

Spending Charts

Visual breakdowns by category, month, and year. Spot trends and outliers at a glance — whether you're tracking business expenses, a side hustle, or just your monthly budget.

📄

CSV & PDF Export

Export your expenses and mileage logs as CSV for spreadsheets or PDF for sharing. Filter by date range before exporting to get exactly the data you need.

☁

iCloud Sharing & Peer Collaboration

Share a read-only live view with your accountant, or invite a trusted person to collaborate in real time — like iOS Notes, but for your finances. All powered by iCloud, no extra apps needed.

🔒

Local Storage, Encryption & Biometric Lock

All data is stored in an encrypted SQLite database using iOS Data Protection. Lock the app with Face ID or a passcode for an extra layer of security. Your finances are never sent to any third-party server — iCloud is the only optional connection, entirely within Apple's ecosystem.

AI & Intelligence

Private AI that actually works

Every intelligent feature runs entirely on your device — no internet required, no data sent to third-party servers.

✨

AI Receipt Scanning

On-device OCR powered by ML Kit extracts text, then a DistilBERT NER model identifies the merchant, grand total, tax, tip, and date with per-field confidence scoring. High-confidence fields auto-fill the form; low-confidence ones prompt you to confirm.

🎤

Voice Commands

Log expenses hands-free. Say "add $45 for lunch at Chipotle" and an on-device BERT intent classifier parses the amount, merchant, and category — then silently creates the record with a 5-second undo window.

💬

Ask Your Expenses

Type a plain-English question like "what did I spend on food in April?" and an on-device Llama model translates it to a SQL query against your local database. Tax-aware answers built in — 50% meals rule, TCJA entertainment limits, per-year IRS mileage rates.

🧠

Smart Auto-Categorization

Summa Ledger learns from your habits. It recognises 100+ common merchants out of the box and gets smarter with every expense you save — automatically suggesting the right category so you rarely have to pick one manually.

🔒 All AI models — receipt understanding, voice intent, and natural language — are downloaded to your device and run using Apple's on-device ML frameworks. Your financial data is never sent to third-party servers. The only optional cloud connection is iCloud — for backup, accountant sharing, and peer collaboration.
