Atlas Ledger

Built for the way you work

Every feature is designed for freelancers and small business owners who need clarity, speed, and complete privacy.

Core

Expense Management

The foundation — fast, flexible, and fully offline.

📋

Expense Logging

Add expenses in seconds. Set the amount, category, date, and notes — then get back to work. Full search and filter across your entire history.

🏷

Custom Categories

Organize expenses using built-in Schedule C categories or create your own. Every category maps cleanly to tax deduction types.

📷

Receipt Capture & OCR

Photograph a receipt and the app reads the merchant, date, and amount for you. Attach photos directly to any expense for a complete audit trail.

🔍

Smart Search & Filters

Instantly find any expense by keyword, category, date range, or amount. Multi-select filters let you slice your data any way you need.

Mileage

Never miss a deduction

The IRS allows a standard mileage deduction for business travel. Atlas Ledger makes it effortless to claim every mile.

🚗

Trip Logging

Log any trip with start/end locations and distance. Add a purpose and client — everything you need for a clean IRS-compliant mileage log.

📈

Automatic Deduction Calculation

Atlas Ledger applies the current IRS standard mileage rate automatically. See your total deduction value update in real time as you log trips.

🖽

Mileage Reports

Export a complete mileage log in CSV or PDF — formatted exactly as tax preparers and accountants expect to receive it.

🎯

Purpose Tracking

Tag each trip as Business, Medical, Charity, or Personal. Different trip types use different IRS rates — Atlas Ledger handles the math.

Reports & Sharing

Clarity at tax time and beyond

Powerful reports and seamless accountant sharing make year-end easy.

📊

Spending Charts

Visual breakdowns by category, month, and year. Spot trends and outliers at a glance with interactive charts designed for small business finances.

📄

CSV & PDF Export

Export your expenses and mileage logs as CSV for spreadsheets or PDF for sharing. Filter by date range before exporting to get exactly the data you need.

☁

Accountant Sharing via iCloud

Share a read-only live view of your books with your accountant using iCloud. They see what they need — nothing more, and no extra apps required.

🔒

100% Local Storage

All data is stored in an encrypted SQLite database on your device using iOS Data Protection. Your finances are never uploaded to any third-party server.
