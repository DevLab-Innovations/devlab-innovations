About Us

Software that puts you in control

DevLab Innovations is an independent software studio building privacy-first tools for anyone who wants control over their finances — no matter how they earn.

Our Mission

We build tools you can trust

Most financial apps treat your data as a product. They require accounts, sync to external servers, and monetize your spending habits. We think that's wrong.

At DevLab Innovations, every product we build starts from a simple principle: your data belongs to you. That means local-first storage, no required accounts, and no third-party data sharing — ever.

Summa Ledger is our flagship app — built for freelancers, side hustlers, small business owners, and everyday people who want powerful expense tracking without the complexity or the privacy trade-offs of traditional software.

🔒

Privacy by Design

Every feature is built with privacy as a first-class constraint, not an afterthought.

⚡

Fast & Offline-First

Local SQLite means the app is instant — even with no internet connection.

🎯

Built for Real Work

Designed around real life — whether you're self-employed, running a side hustle, or just keeping tabs on your budget.

Our Values

What we stand for

Four principles guide every decision we make — from architecture to app store description copy.

🔒

Privacy

We will never send your financial data to third-party servers. Local-first is a hard constraint — the only optional cloud connection is Apple's iCloud, for backup and sharing on your terms.

★

Simplicity

Complexity is the enemy of consistency. We cut features that don't earn their place, so the features that remain work beautifully and reliably.

📋

Honesty

No dark patterns. No subscription traps. Clear pricing, clear data practices, and a changelog that tells you exactly what changed and why.

🛠

Craftsmanship

We obsess over the details — accessibility, performance, edge cases. Software built to last is better for users and better for the environment.

Our Product

Summa Ledger

The expense tracker built for everyone — from freelancers and side hustlers to anyone who wants to take control of their spending.

📋

Expense & Mileage Tracking

Log expenses and business trips with the detail required for accurate tax filings — without the friction that makes most people give up.

📊

Reports & Tax Exports

Generate reports formatted for Schedule C, export mileage logs, and share your books with your accountant — all from your iPhone.

🔓

No Account Required

Open the app and start tracking. There's no sign-up flow, no email verification, and no personal data collection required to use any feature.

☁

iCloud Backup & Sharing

Optionally back up to your personal iCloud account. Share a read-only view with your accountant — entirely within Apple's privacy ecosystem.
