Why Summa Ledger

AI-powered.
Completely private.

Every intelligent feature runs entirely on your device. No third-party cloud, no tracking — with optional iCloud for backup and sharing.

✨

On-Device AI Receipts

Snap a receipt and on-device AI reads the merchant, amount, date, and category automatically. Powered by a DistilBERT model running entirely on your iPhone — your receipts never touch a server.

🎤

Voice & Natural Language

Say "add $45 for lunch" to log an expense in seconds. Ask "how much did I spend on travel last month?" and get a real answer — processed entirely on your device, no internet required.

🧠

Gets Smarter Over Time

Summa Ledger learns your spending patterns — auto-suggesting categories, detecting recurring subscriptions, and recognizing your favourite merchants without you lifting a finger.

🔒

Private by Default. iCloud When You Want.

No account required. No telemetry. No third-party servers — ever. When you're ready, optionally enable iCloud backup, share your books with your accountant, or collaborate with a trusted person — all within Apple's ecosystem.
